Star Burst Smoothie

Prep time: 20 mins

Yield: 1 litre

Ingredients





Steps

  1. Skin carambola and slice off the flesh from the core like you would an apple, but you can also leave the skin on.
  2. Combine with other peeled fruits, ginger and orange juice in blender or Nutribullet.
  3. Refrigirate for 15 min
  4. Serve.
