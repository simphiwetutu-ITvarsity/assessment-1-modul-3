Chocolate Mousse Cake

Prep time: 3 hours

Yield: 600 grams

Ingredients




Steps

  1. Pour the chocolate chips into a large bowl. Microwave the cream, pour over the chips and let sit for a few minutes and stir.
  2. Whip up the remaining cream. Just plain cream. Do not add sugar.
  3. Gently fold in a bit of the warm chocolate ganache to temper the cream. Now add the remaining ganache to the cream
  4. Layer chocolate graham crackers, chocolate mousse and repeat, ending with chocolate mousse.
  5. Garnish with chopped chocolate chips that you set aside earlier. Chill for 2 hours or overnight. Can be made 3 days in advance
  6. Serve
