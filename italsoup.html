Jamaican Ital Soup

Prep time: 2 Hours

Yield: 4

Ingredients






Steps

  1. First wash 1 cup of split peas and place in a large pot
  2. Then pour 8-10 cups of water into the pot and cook split peas for 30mins until tender.
  3. Then add your Garlic cloves, onions, spring onions, thyme and grated ginger.
  4. Add 1 tsp black ground pepper, 1 tsp Pimento , 1 tsp sea salt
  5. Then stir and bring to a boil
  6. Then add 1 cup of Sweet potatoes,1 1/2 cup of pumpkin
  7. 1 cup of cho cho,1/4 cup of white potatoes,1 cup of okra,1 cup of Carrots
  8. Then add 2 cups coconut milk and stir well, 1 cup of callaloo, 1 cup of spanish/basil/kale, 1 scotch bonnet pepper
  9. Stir well and simmer 40 mins
